How I really feel when making this webpage

My name is Anthony Hitt, I am fifteen years old, with my birthday on March 30th, 2001. I am currently a sophomore in Summit Rainier High School.

It was during the summer of 2014 that I actually began coding. It started when my older brother, a year older then me, was introduced to Codecademy by his teacher. After being told I should start coding but with his support, I decided to make an account on codecademy and begin coding. While I did end up expanding to other coding sites, such as khan academy & freecodecamp, I struggled to get further from HTML & CSS. I decided to come to computer science, hoping that the education here would help turn that difficulty around. Things are looking on the up and up!

Summit Rainier has been an extremely important part of my coding career. I joined Summit in the year 2015, a year before I created this webpage. Here, they have provided a unique educational experience, unlike any other school i've been to, and I am very thankful for that. In Summit Rainier, computers are an integral part of your high school life, which makes a computer class like this one MUCH easier. With the advantage of a school which supports online self-development, coding became a part of the work I do here.
